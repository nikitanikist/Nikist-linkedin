# Outreach Machine — LinkedIn Growth Engine

Execute systematic LinkedIn outreach that books meetings. This is where content and profile work converts into actual revenue.

## Prerequisites

1. ICP is defined (ICP Builder skill)
2. Profile is optimized (Profile Optimizer skill)
3. Content is running (Content Engine skill — at least 2 weeks of posts)

From the course: "Outreach without an optimized profile is like cold calling with a broken phone."

## Volume Targets

Daily minimums from the course:
- **20-25 new connection requests** (with personalized notes)
- **20-25 first messages** to new connections
- **10-15 follow-ups** to non-responders
- **Total: 600-800 outreach touchpoints per month**

Weekly targets:
- 140-175 messages sent
- 56-70 new connections added
- 3-5 discovery calls booked

## The Outreach System (5 Steps)

### Step 1: Build Prospect List

Using the ICP from icp-builder, find prospects via:
1. **Sales Navigator search** with saved filters
2. **Profile viewers** (warmest leads — already interested)
3. **Post engagers** (people who liked/commented on relevant content)
4. **Event attendees** from LinkedIn events
5. **Competitor followers**

Target: 600 qualified prospects to start.

### Step 2: Send Connection Requests

**Connection request note (300 char max):**

Template A (Problem-based):
"Hi [Name], I noticed you're [working on / leading / building] [specific thing from their profile]. I help [ICP] with [specific problem]. Would love to connect and share ideas."

Template B (Mutual interest):
"Hi [Name], saw your post about [topic]. Really resonated with me — I work with [ICP] on [related area]. Let's connect!"

Template C (Trigger-based):
"Hi [Name], congrats on [recent achievement/funding/hire]. I work with [similar companies] to [result]. Would love to connect."

**Critical Rule**: ALWAYS personalize the first line. The course emphasizes: "Copy-paste outreach is dead. One personalized line = 3x response rate."

### Step 3: First Message (after connection accepted)

Wait 1-2 days after connection, then send:

Template 1 (Value-first):
"Hey [Name], thanks for connecting! I put together a [resource/framework/guide] on [topic relevant to their pain]. Want me to share it?"

Template 2 (Question-based):
"Hey [Name], curious — what's the biggest challenge you're facing with [topic relevant to ICP]? I work with a lot of [their role/industry] and keep hearing [common pain]."

Template 3 (Profile viewer):
"Hey [Name], noticed you checked out my profile — what brought you there? Happy to help if you're looking into [your area of expertise]."

### Step 4: Follow-Up Sequence

If no reply:

**Day 3 — Soft nudge:**
"Hey [Name], just floating this back up! I know things get busy. [Rephrase the value prop in 1 sentence]. Would love to chat if it's relevant."

**Day 7 — Value add:**
"Hey [Name], thought of you when I saw [relevant article/stat/post]. [Share a quick insight]. Let me know if [topic] is something you're working on."

**Day 14 — Direct ask:**
"Hey [Name], I'll keep it short — I help [ICP] [achieve result]. If that's on your radar, I'd love to do a quick 15-min call to see if I can help. If not, no worries at all!"

**After 3 follow-ups with no response**: Move on. Don't spam.

### Step 5: Discovery Call Conversion

When someone replies positively, move to a call:

**Booking message:**
"Awesome! Would love to jump on a quick 15-min call to learn more about [their situation] and see if I can help. Here's my calendar: [link]. Or let me know a time that works for you."

**Discovery Call Framework (10 questions):**
1. Tell me about your business/role
2. What are you currently doing for [your area]?
3. What's working well?
4. What's the biggest challenge?
5. How long has this been a problem?
6. What have you tried to fix it?
7. What would success look like for you?
8. What's the timeline?
9. Who else is involved in this decision?
10. What's the budget range you're considering?

**Record calls with Fireflies** for transcription and follow-up.

4. For existing connections, send first message
5. Track responses and follow up on schedule

## Budget Conversation Framework

When prospects ask about pricing:

1. "Before I give you a number, let me understand what you need" (ask scope questions)
2. "Based on what you've described, companies like yours typically invest [range]"
3. "The ROI is [specific number] — for every ₹1 you invest, you get [X] back"
4. "Would it help if I showed you a case study of someone in a similar situation?"
5. "What budget range were you considering?"

## Conversion Metrics

- Cold outreach reply rate: 1-3% (normal)
- Warm lead reply rate: 20-30%
- Profile viewer reply rate: 15-25%
- Reply → Meeting conversion: 30-40%
- Meeting → Deal conversion: 20-30%
- Target: 10-30 meetings/month with consistent effort

## Anti-Patterns

- Don't copy-paste the same message to everyone — personalize first line minimum
- Don't pitch in the connection request — build rapport first
- Don't send more than 3 follow-ups — it's spam after that
- Don't automate before you've tested manually — find what converts first
- Don't outreach without content running — your profile needs social proof
- Don't ignore warm leads (profile viewers, post engagers) to chase cold ones
- Don't be pushy — "no worries" should be your favorite phrase
